Red Cedar Dance Group Committee Members

盛晨 · Cindy

红杉树青年舞团团长 · Troupe Director

红杉树青年舞团创始人, 悉尼大学商学硕士。自幼学习中国民族民间舞，拥有十余年澳洲本地舞蹈编排、舞台表演及舞团统筹管理经验。热心公益, 致力于以舞蹈为载体传播中华优秀传统文化，助力中澳文化艺术交流。。

Founder of Red Cedar Dance Group, Master of Commerce from the University of Sydney. Trained in Chinese folk dances from young, with over a decade of local Australian experience in choreography, stage performance and troupe administration. Passionate about public welfare, she devotes herself to promoting traditional Chinese culture through dance and facilitating cultural exchanges between China and Australia.

殷若茗 · Victoria Yin

红杉树青年舞团执委 · Executive Committee Member

毕业于澳大利亚国立大学。三岁起系统学习中国古典舞、芭蕾舞及民族民间舞，舞龄二十二年。 在中国国内多次参加省市级大型文艺演出及赛事，具备舞蹈编排、教学及大型活动策划能力。

During her studies at the ANU, she was a member of the ANU Cheerleading Team and claimed the gold medal at Australia’s National University Cheerleading Championships. She previously served as Vice President of the ACT Chinese Students & Scholars Association and directed major Mid‑Autumn Festival and National Day galas.

朱泓玉 · Chelsey Zhu

红杉树青年舞团财务总监 · Financial Officer

注册会计师，澳国立大学（ANU）金融管理硕士及沃隆港大学（UOW）会计学士。

自幼学习中国民族舞，考级参加比赛，从小学至大学期间始终加入校舞蹈队，积极参与各类文艺演出及文化活动策划。现从事会计专业工作，业余时间坚持舞蹈热爱，参与舞台表演和文化推广活动。希望与舞团伙伴们一道，通过舞蹈展现中国民族民间舞的独特魅力，传递中华文化之美。

A CPA, and holds a Master of Financial Management from The Australian National University and a Bachelor of Accounting from the University of Wollongong.

She began training in Chinese ethnic dance at a young age, undertaking formal dance examinations and participating in competitions. From primary school through university, she was an active member of school dance teams and regularly participated in stage performances, cultural events, and arts program planning activities

While pursuing a professional career in accounting and finance, she continues to pursue her passion for dance, dedicating her spare time to stage performances and cultural promotion initiatives. Through dance, she hopes to showcase the unique beauty and artistic richness of Chinese ethnic and folk dance, and to share the elegance and diversity of Chinese culture with broader audiences.
