Our Story

红杉舞蹈队成立于1997年，由一群热爱舞蹈、充满活力的年轻女性组成。 多年来，我们积极参与堪培拉及周边地区的各类社区活动， 通过舞蹈传递文化、连接社区、丰富多元文化生活。

The Red Cedar Dancing Group was founded in 1997 by a group of passionate young women who love dance. Since then, we have actively participated in community and cultural events across Canberra, using dance as a universal language to promote multiculturalism, foster understanding, and enrich community life through high-quality performances.
