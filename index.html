Welcome

Welcome to Red Cedar Dance Group - a community of passionate dancers who brings cultural stories to life through powerful movements, energetic rhythms and unforgettable performances

Our passion is rooted in heritage, fueled by creativity, and shared with everyone who joins us.

From festivals to community celebrations, our dances honor tradition while embracing fresh, modern expression.

Every performance is a heartbeat of our culture — alive, dynamic, and full of joy. Whether you’re here to dance, learn, or simply experience the energy, you’re part of our story the moment you arrive.


Come move with us. Celebrate with us. Grow with us.
We bring people closer and build bridges of friendship and love.


欢迎来到红杉树舞蹈团！ 我们是一支充满活力的舞蹈团队，用强劲的动作、鲜明的节奏和富有感染力的表演，将文化故事呈现得鲜活生动。

我们的热情源于传统，绽放于创意，并愿与每一位来到这里的人分享。 无论是在节庆、文化活动，还是社区舞台上，我们的舞蹈都在传承文化的同时，融入现代的表达方式。

每一次表演，都是文化的脉动——鲜活、热烈、充满生命力。 无论你是舞者、学习者，还是观众，从踏入这里的那一刻起，你就成为我们故事的一部分。


一起舞动，一起庆祝，一起成长。

我们让人与人更紧密相连，搭起友爱与温暖的桥梁。
