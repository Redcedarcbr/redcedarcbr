More About Our Performances

The Red Cedar Dance Group continues to share vibrant, cross-cultural performances across Canberra and beyond. Each event blends tradition, creativity, and community spirit.

Canberra Multicultural Performances

In 2025, the Red Cedar Dance Group proudly returned to the Canberra Multicultural Festival, bringing a vibrant blend of traditional and contemporary Chinese dance to one of the city’s most celebrated cultural events. Our performance showcased the elegance of classical movement alongside energetic modern choreography, reflecting the spirit of cultural exchange and unity.
